Run continuity and canon lint checks on the Novel World Wiki.

Usage: /wiki-lint

Checks:
1. Orphans and broken links
2. Timeline contradictions
3. Character continuity breaks
4. Setup/payoff gaps and unresolved threads
5. Alias collisions and canon drift
6. Sparse pages with low link density

Produce a markdown report and ask whether to save it to `wiki/lint-report.md`.
